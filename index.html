What Happens In Class

01

Rowing

Every stroke on the rower activites 85% of your body's muscles to help you improve endurance ,strength and power.Rowing is a versatile and effective workout that can be an integral part of your gym routine, helping you achieve your fitness goals and improve overall health

02

Cardio

Whether you walk,jog or run,you'll go at your own pace based on your fitness level.We also have bikes and striders available as alternates to the treadmill.It can help reduce body fat and increase lean muscle mass when combined with a balanced diet.It is an essential part of maintaining overall health and fitness.

About

"Welcome to ShapeUp, your one-stop destination for all things fitness. We're more than just a store; we're your partner in achieving your health and wellness goals. At ShapeUp, we offer a carefully curated selection of top-quality gym equipment, stylish activewear, and cutting-edge accessories to support your fitness journey. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting, our expert staff is here to guide you in choosing the right products that match your needs and aspirations. Our mission is to make fitness accessible and enjoyable for everyone, which is why we offer competitive prices and frequent promotions. We believe in fostering a sense of community, where fitness enthusiasts of all levels can connect, inspire, and motivate each other. Quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction are at the core of everything we do. ShapeUp is where your fitness dreams become a reality. Join us on this incredible journey and experience the difference today."
